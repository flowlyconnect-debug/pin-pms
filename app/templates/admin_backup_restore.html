This action will overwrite the current database.

  • Sessions and recent edits made after this backup was taken will be lost.
  • If your account does not exist in the backup, you will be logged out on the next request.
  • A "pre-restore" safe-copy of the current state is taken automatically and saved alongside other backups, so the change can be reverted by restoring that file.
